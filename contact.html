ABOUT US

Meet Gathoni

Gathoni lives in Kitisuru with her husband Ivan, their son Alvin and their two fish, Doha and Nemo. Professionally, she is a lawyer practising at Caplann and Sandton.

In 2021, she started Tots & Tois following the birth of her son. What started as a side hustle has since grown to be a key supplier for baby clothes, accessories and maternity wear. Some of Gathoni's hobbies including making cinnamon rolls, travelling with her family and decorating spaces.
